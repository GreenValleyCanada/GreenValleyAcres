Green Valley Acres

Local food & poultry grown with love

Green Valley Acres is located near Medicine Hat, Alberta. We lovingly grow our Saskatoon Berry orchard, fresh fruits, and vegetables when seasonally available. We also have a passion for poultry and currently raise a variety of chickens, pheasants, peacocks, guineas, and turkeys.

Our farm was owned and operated by Brittany and Mark until July 2020 when we welcomed Baby James to our farming family.
